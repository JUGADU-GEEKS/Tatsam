About Us

Our Mission

Tatsam, the Hindi Society of NSUT, is dedicated to promoting Hindi language and Indian culture within our campus and beyond. We believe in the rich heritage of our language and work towards ensuring it remains relevant and appreciated by the younger generation.

What We Do

  • Organize literary events including poetry recitals, debates, and storytelling sessions
  • Host cultural celebrations during festivals
  • Publish a quarterly magazine featuring student writings
  • Conduct Hindi language workshops for beginners
